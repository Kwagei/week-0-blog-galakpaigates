The Heavenly Parents Holy Community (HPHC) is an International Organization, divided into to Sub-sets: Family Federation for World Peace and Unification (FFWPU) and the Universal Peace Federation (UPF).

The Heavenly Parents Holy Community has its Liberia Headquaters at Du-port Road Market Behind the Mosque, Paynesville, Montserrado County.

The Family Federation for World Peace & Unification (FFWPU) mainly deals with the Internal Members of the Heavenly Parents Holy Community, this Sub-set mainly has to do with the Church.

The Holy Marriage Blessing of 30 Couples


Buchannan City, Grand Bassa County

The Family Federation for World Peace & Unification hosted a Holy Blessing Ceremony in the City of Buchannan, Grand Bassa County, Liberia. The Ceremony was attended by 30 Couples whom renewed their vow and started a new beginning as a Couple and a Family.

The Universal Peace Federation (UPF) is the second sub-set of the Heavenly Parents Holy Community (HPHC). It has many auxilary providential activities, that are mainly focused on Peace Building, such as the Youth & Students for Peace (YSP), Inter-Religious Association for Peace and Development (IAPD), Women Federation for World Peace (WFWP), and many others.
